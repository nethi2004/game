GAME PLAY!

Here are some things you should know before playing:D

The ships are already randomized however you can
move/customize any of the "ships" on the user board
to your liking to place the battleships!

(they aren't made in groups so you can move them any
way you want and group which ever you want)

The game starts after you make your first move on the PC board.

BEWARE THO! once you start the game you can't
change the positions of your ships


Goodluck girl>.<
hope you win!


More info of the setup :
Rules of the game :

There are 17 ships on each board.

The player clicks at random hoping to take
the opponents ships down (the PC's board)

You can change the placement of your ships

When you start your turn the game will automatically start!
A player will only win if they take out
all of the opponents ships. (17 ships)
